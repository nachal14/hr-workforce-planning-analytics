# HR Workforce Planning & Attrition Analytics | Power BI Project

> **ZoomCharts July 2026 Report Challenge Entry** — Moving past surface-level headcount metrics to uncover hidden drivers of attrition, talent bottlenecks, and organizational performance gaps.

---

## 📌 Executive Summary
Standard HR dashboards often mask critical operational risks behind healthy company-wide averages. This project delivers an interactive Power BI workforce analytics report designed to help business leaders diagnose structural issues in hiring, retention, and performance rather than treating symptoms.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* **The Department Divide:** Sales attrition sits at a critical **28.72%**—more than 10x Finance (2.74%). This proves the organization faces a department-specific retention crisis, not a company-wide one. Meanwhile, Finance's performance dropped for two consecutive years despite having the lowest attrition (comfortable, but unproductive).
* **The Career Ceiling:** **33% of all terminations (48 of 144)** happen in the 2–5 year tenure band. This points directly to a stalled internal career ladder and mid-career growth stagnation rather than day-one burnout.
* **The Reorg Double-Hit:** **86% of involuntary exits** stem from company restructuring rather than performance issues. However, the mid-layer continues to carry the highest post-reorg attrition count, indicating they absorbed the heaviest operational friction.
* **The Hiring Paradox:** Data & Analytics faces a severe recruitment bottleneck, averaging **55+ days to hire with a 33% success rate**, while **88% of the talent/department budget sits unutilized**—highlighting a disconnect between financial planning and recruitment execution.
* **Performance-Engagement Divergence:** Company-wide performance indices have steadily declined since 2024 despite flat engagement scores, signaling emerging workload or process friction.

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

1. **Targeted Mid-Tenure Retention:** Implement structured career-pathing check-ins, internal mobility frameworks, and targeted retention plans specifically for the 2–5 year tenure band to eliminate growth stagnation.
2. **Streamline Talent Acquisition:** Overhaul the recruitment funnel for Data & Analytics and HR to cut down the 55+ day turnaround time, while auditing budget metrics to fix severe capital under-utilization.
3. **Proactive Performance Interventions:** Launch immediate manager check-ins for high-risk employees and execute targeted performance-improvement programs in departments like Finance and Marketing where performance significantly lags stability.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Stack & Approach
* **Tool:** Power BI (Advanced DAX, Custom Visuals via ZoomCharts)
* **Data Modeling:** Star Schema architecture linking employee demographics, employee hire/terminate, performance indexes, tenure tracking, and workforce target.
* **Core Techniques:** Cohort/tenure segmentation, Time intelligence,cross-filtering for department-level drill-downs, and automated variance analysis, Composite risk scoring via weighted normalization.
